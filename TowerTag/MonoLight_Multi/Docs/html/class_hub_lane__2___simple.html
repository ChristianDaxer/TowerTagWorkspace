Detailed Description

HubLane_2_Simple

  • simple approach to represent Lanes with 3 Pillars (SpawnPillar, tagNGoPillar (TutorialPillar to learn how to claim & Teleport) and readyPillar (Pillar to represent that the Player is ready to start/play the Game))
  • the spawnPillar is the entry point for the local Player (spawnPillar-OccupyingPlayerChanged as Entry point for the Lane)
  • a remote Player could Enter the Lane on any of the 3 Pillars (because he could teleport before we connect)
